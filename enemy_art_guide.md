# 🍌 Banana Survivors: Enemy Bestiary & Art Guide

The players are 256x256 assets (representing 42x42 in-game pixels). Use the guide below to create consistent assets for all current enemy types.

---

## 🟢 Small / Common Enemies
These move directly towards the player (`seek` AI) and deal damage on contact.

- **Maggot** (28px) — *170x170 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Slow, low HP. Wiggles as it moves.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A pale, segment-ribbed larva with a tiny, angry face. Sticky or slimy texture.
- **Mold Fly** (22px) — *134x134 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Fast, fragile. Bounces/flaps quickly.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A tiny insect with iridescent, mold-textured wings. Large bulbous eyes.
- **Rot Slug** (40x24px) — *244x146 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Very slow but sturdy. Wiggles rhythmically.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A thick, orange-tinted slug with trail details and eye-stalks. Leathery, wet look.
- **Spore Moth** (32px) — *195x195 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Balanced speed/health. Flaps steadily.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A moth with powdery, purple-veined wings. Spores falling from its body.
- **Vicious Wasp** (26px) — *158x158 asset*
  - **Behavior**: High speed, low health. Aggressive.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A bright yellow-black striped hornet with a sharp, elongated stinger.

---

## 🟠 Elite & Middleweight Enemies
Tougher, larger, and often have special properties like glows or higher elite stats.

- **Fungal Horror** (54px) — *330x330 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Elite mushroom. Slow, very high HP. Rocks as it moves.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A gnarled red mushroom cap with parasitic spores or "limbs". Toothy mouth.
- **Armored Beetle** (38px) — *232x232 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Slow, tanky. High knockback resistance.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A dark moss-green beetle with a heavy, plated carapace (like a tank).
- **Giant Centipede** (45px) — *274x274 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Large and tough. Quick-wiggle animation.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A long, segmented creature with hundreds of tiny legs. Multi-eyed head.
- **Charger Scout** (84px) — *512x512 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Elite version of the Charger. Telegraphs with a red flash, then dashes quickly.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A medium-sized, aggressive-looking creature with small bull horns. Gray/metallic skin.

---

## 🔴 Bosses & Titans (Mega-Sized)
Massive threats with complex AI and screen-filling attacks.

- **Eldritch Peel** (70px) — *426x426 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Giant boss. Large HP pool, simple seeking.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A rotten banana peel that has split open to reveal an eldritch skull and tentacles.
- **Storm Titan** (168px) — *1024x1024 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Elite giant. Telegraphs with blue flashes, then fires a chain lightning bolt at the player and nearby allies.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A massive, cloud-colored brute pulsing with lightning. Floating sparks and static.
- **Glacial Behemoth** (210px) — *1280x1280 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Boss giant. Expands an icy ring warning, then freezes and damages everything in a massive radius.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A mountainous ice creature with frozen spikes and deep blue core. Brittle, crystalline body.
- **Magma Lord** (147px) — *896x896 asset*
  - **Behavior**: Elite giant. Leaps to a target location, slams down (shake!), and leaves behind burning magma pools.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A volcanic entity with a molten lava chest. Fire vents on its shoulders.
- **Behemoth Charger** (420px) — *~2560x2560 asset (High Res)*
  - **Behavior**: The 10x player-size boss. Huge telegraph (flashing red), then charges halfway across the map with a massive screen shake.
  - **Art Suggestion**: A colossal, rhino-like beast with massive horns. Dark gray, rock-like skin with glowing red "anger" veins.
