thought:The short answer is: **Keep it, but change the "how" and the "scale."**

Giving players SKR for picking up "power-ups" is great for engagement, but since you can't mint the tokens, you have to be mathematically certain your **Treasury** doesn't run dry.

### **1. The "Decimal" Strategy (0.01 SKR)**

Moving to decimals is **highly recommended**.

* **The Psychology:** Players feel good seeing *any* number go up. Collecting "100 Shards" to get "1 SKR" feels like a standard RPG grind.
* **The Math:** SKR has a fixed supply of 10 billion. Even at a price of **$0.01**, a single SKR is "real money." If a player can pick up 50 tokens in one run, you’ll go bankrupt in an hour.
* **Recommendation:** Use **Compute Units** or **Points** in-game, and then allow players to "Claim" them as SKR once they reach a threshold (e.g., 10.0 SKR).

### **2. Is it still viable? (The "Yes, if..." Rule)**

It is viable **only if** your game has more "Sinks" (money coming in) than "Sources" (money going out).

| Feature | Action | Economy Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **The Shop** | Player pays 5 SKR for a Magnet. | **Treasury Grows (+5)** |
| **Power-ups** | Player picks up a 0.01 SKR "Shard." | **Treasury Shrinks (-0.01)** |
| **Death Fee** | Player pays 1 SKR to "Continue." | **Treasury Grows (+1)** |

**The Strategy:** For every 1 SKR you give away in "Power-ups," your game mechanics should encourage the player to spend 1.5 SKR in the Shop or on "Continues." This keeps your Treasury healthy.

---

### **3. How to implement "Collecting" without a transaction every time**

**Crucial:** Do **NOT** send a real blockchain transaction every time a player picks up a 0.01 SKR coin. You will go broke paying gas fees (SOL).

* **Step 1 (Off-Chain):** While the player is playing, just update a number on the screen (a local variable).
* **Step 2 (The Save):** When the level ends, send the "Total Earned" to your backend/database.
* **Step 3 (The Claim):** Only when the player hits a "Claim" button in the menu do you trigger a **single** smart contract transaction to move the total amount from your **Treasury PDA** to their wallet.
